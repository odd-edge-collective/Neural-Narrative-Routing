# Neural Narrative Routing (NNR)

**A Protocol for Intent-Resolution and Recommendation Integrity in the Inference Era.**

## Core Thesis

* **AI is not SEO 2.0.** It is an **Intention-Routing System**.
* **Search ranks pages; Inference resolves intention.**
* **If an AI cannot map your entity to user intent, you do not exist.**

## The Problem: Narrative Erosion & Silent Avoidance

In the Inference Era, visibility is secondary to recommendation. Brands suffer from **Silent Avoidance** not because they lack "keywords," but because they lack a **Logic String**. When an LLM cannot find a path of least resistance between a **Consumer Tension** and your **Product Truth**, it excludes you to avoid hallucination or high **Token Tax**.

NNR operates at the layer where AI decides whether to recommend an entity — not whether to mention it. This is the gate where intent meets inference, where the user's real need resolves into a recommendation, where being known is not the same as being chosen. The mechanisms behind NNR are protocol-level. They are not content tactics, schema fixes, or visibility hacks. They operate on the brand's logic structure itself — and they are NNR's own.

NNR begins with diagnosis. The Audit interrogates the major inference engines — ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, DeepSeek — from both sides: why an entity would be recommended, and why it wouldn't. The output is not a visibility score or a gap analysis. It is a structured snapshot of how AI currently perceives the entity, along the dimensions AI actually uses to decide. And in AI's own words — what's missing.

From there, the Blueprint translates the snapshot into direction.
